Age Requirement

PlayStella is intended for users aged 13 and older. If you are under 13 years of age, please do not use this site without parental consent and supervision.

Information We Collect

PlayStella collects minimal information to provide you with the best gaming experience:

1. Automatically Collected Information

  • IP Address: When you visit our site, your IP address is automatically logged by our hosting provider (Netlify) for security, performance, and anonymous analytics purposes. We do not directly access, store, or use your IP address to identify you personally.
  • Anonymous Analytics Data: Our hosting provider collects server-side analytics including pageviews, unique visitors (counted by IP address), geographic location (country/region level), pages visited, and bandwidth usage. This data is anonymized and aggregated — no individual user tracking occurs.

2. Browser Storage (Local Storage)

We use browser local storage (similar to cookies) to save your game progress locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and includes:

  • Last Play Date: To enforce the once-per-day play limit (stored as date string, e.g., "Fri Oct 22 2025")
  • Best Time: Your personal best score (stored as numeric value, e.g., "15.7")
  • Streak Count: Number of consecutive days played (stored as numeric value)
  • Last Game Stats: Your most recent game time and action count for sharing purposes
  • Last Game History: A record of your last game's action outcomes (e.g., whether actions were "good," "bad," or "neutral") used to generate the visual grid when sharing results. This is stored as an anonymous array with no personal identifiers.

Important: This information is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties. The action history contains no personal information—only generic game outcomes like "pet: good" or "treat: bad" that are used to create the colorful share grid.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Payment or financial information
  • Social media profiles or account credentials
  • Precise geolocation data
  • Personal identifiers or tracking cookies
  • Behavioral tracking or analytics data
  • Any information that could identify you personally

How We Use Information

  • Game Functionality: Local storage data enables core game features like daily play limits, score tracking, streak counters, and the visual share feature
  • Share Feature: Action history is used solely to generate the colorful grid display when you share your results. This data remains on your device and is only included in share text if you choose to share.
  • Site Security: IP addresses logged by our hosting provider help prevent abuse and ensure site security
  • Anonymous Analytics: Server-side analytics help us understand site traffic patterns, popular pages, and overall usage trends to improve the game experience. All analytics are anonymous and aggregated — we cannot and do not track individual users.

We do not:

  • Sell, rent, or share any data with third parties
  • Use data for advertising or marketing purposes
  • Track your behavior across websites
  • Create user profiles or accounts
  • Use cookies for tracking (server-side analytics only)
  • Collect personally identifiable information through analytics
  • Transmit your local game data to our servers

Third-Party Services

PlayStella uses the following third-party services:

Hosting Provider (Netlify)

Our site is hosted on Netlify, which collects:

  • IP addresses: Logged automatically for security, performance, and analytics purposes
  • Server logs: Include access times, pages visited, browser type, and referring sources
  • Anonymous analytics: Server-side analytics track pageviews, unique visitors (by IP address), geographic locations, and bandwidth usage

Important: Netlify's analytics are server-side only and do not use cookies, JavaScript tracking, or collect personally identifiable information. All data is anonymized and aggregated. IP addresses are used only to count unique visitors and determine geographic regions, not to identify individuals.

Learn more: Netlify Privacy Policy | How Netlify Analytics Works

Content Delivery Networks (CDNs)

We use public CDNs (unpkg.com, Tailwind CSS) to load open-source libraries. These are standard, read-only resources that do not collect personal information.

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Game data is stored only on your device in your browser's local storage
  • No Server Storage: We do not store any game data, scores, or personal information on our servers
  • Data Control: You can clear all local storage data at any time through your browser settings
  • Action History Privacy: Your game action history (used for the share feature) never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it via the share button

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view your locally stored game data through your browser's developer tools (press F12, go to "Application" or "Storage" tab, and view "Local Storage")
  • Deletion: You can delete all game data by clearing your browser's local storage or cache. Instructions vary by browser—search for "[your browser name] clear local storage"
  • Opt-Out: You can stop using the site at any time, and no further data will be collected
  • Portability: Your data is stored in standard formats (JSON) and can be exported via browser developer tools

International Users

PlayStella is available globally. If you access our site from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be processed in countries where data protection laws may differ from those in your country. However, since we collect no personal information and all game data stays on your device, international data transfer concerns are minimal.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

PlayStella is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has used our site, please note that:

  • No personal information is collected by our game
  • Only anonymous game scores and action histories are stored locally on the device
  • You can clear all data by clearing your browser's local storage
  • No data is transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties

COPPA Compliance: Because PlayStella collects no personal information from users of any age (only anonymous game data stored locally), we meet COPPA's requirements for protecting children's privacy online.
